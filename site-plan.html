Overview

Purpose

Dry Oar is one of the nations best whitewater rafting company. Here, We offer our guests the ability to escape their everyday life for a fun, relaxing, and adventurous white water rafting experience. Founded in 1998 to make it easier to find high-quality white water rafting at an affordable price.

Audience

Looking for some time alone? or an amazing bonding experience? Dry Oar caters to individuals, families, friends, and groups of all sizes. we offer trips that accommodate the little ones to trips that push your endurance to the max!
